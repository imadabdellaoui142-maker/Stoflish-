About the club

Fluency, out loud.

Stoflish began in 2017 with a simple bet: students learn English fastest when they're actually using it.

What started as a handful of conversation meetups at the University of Abou Bekr Belkaïd Tlemcen has grown into a full creative community — weekly workshops, open debates, cultural nights, and a biannual e-magazine that turns members' writing, art, and reporting into finished issues.

Beginners and fluent speakers work side by side. The only requirement is showing up ready to speak.
